Everything You Need to Know

What exactly is +EV betting? ▼

+EV (positive expected value) betting means betting when the odds are mathematically in your favor. For example, if a team has a 55% chance to win but the odds imply only 50%, that's +EV. Over hundreds of bets, this edge compounds into consistent profit. It's not gambling—it's math.

How do the Discord alerts work? ▼

Our system scans 15+ sportsbooks 24/7. When it identifies a +EV bet or arbitrage opportunity, you get an instant Discord notification (usually within 90 seconds). The alert includes: sport, game, bet type, recommended sportsbook, odds, and calculated EV or guaranteed profit percentage.

What's the difference between +EV betting and arbitrage? ▼

+EV betting: You bet when odds are favorable. There's still variance (you can lose individual bets), but the math ensures long-term profit.

Arbitrage: You bet both sides of a game on different sportsbooks to guarantee profit regardless of outcome. Zero risk, but requires accounts on multiple books.

You can start with as little as $500-$1,000, but we recommend $2,000-$5,000 for optimal bankroll management. The more capital you have, the smoother your variance and the faster you can scale. Our education includes full bankroll management and Kelly Criterion guidance.

We recommend having accounts at: DraftKings, FanDuel, BetMGM, Caesars, BetRivers, PointsBet, and as many others as legally available in your state. The more books you have access to, the more +EV opportunities you can capture. We'll guide you through the setup process.

Is this legal? ▼

Yes, 100% legal. We're simply identifying when odds are favorable across legal, regulated sportsbooks. +EV betting and arbitrage are legitimate strategies used by professional bettors. You must be 21+ and betting must be legal in your state.

Can I really guarantee profit with this? ▼

No one can guarantee profit in betting. However, with +EV betting, you have a mathematical edge that ensures long-term profitability over hundreds of bets (assuming proper bankroll management and discipline). Arbitrage opportunities do guarantee profit on individual bets, but they're less common. Our transparent results show exactly what's realistic.

What if I get limited by sportsbooks? ▼

Getting limited is a real risk for successful bettors. Our education includes strategies to avoid getting limited quickly, but it's inevitable if you're consistently winning. The good news: by the time you're limited, you've likely already made significant profit. We'll teach you how to maximize your window.
