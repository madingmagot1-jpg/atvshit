Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

What is +EV betting? ▼
+EV (positive expected value) betting means betting when the odds are mathematically in your favour. Instead of betting on who you think will win, you bet when bookmakers have priced something incorrectly. Over time, this leads to consistent profits.
How much can I make? ▼
Results vary based on your bankroll, time commitment, and how many bookmakers you have access to. Our members typically see 5-15% monthly ROI. The key is treating this like a business, not gambling.
Do I need accounts with all bookies? ▼
No, but the more accounts you have, the more opportunities you'll catch. Most members start with 3-5 bookmakers and expand from there. We'll show you exactly how to set everything up.
How much time does this take? ▼
Most members spend 30-90 minutes per day. You get a notification straight to your phone, check the alert, place the bet, and you're done. No need to manually scan bookmakers for hours like you would doing this yourself.
What sports do you cover? ▼
We cover all major sports: AFL, NRL, cricket, horse racing, NBA, NFL, MLB, soccer, UFC, and more. Opportunities come up across all sports daily.
Will bookies limit my account? ▼
It's possible if you're consistently profitable. We teach strategies to minimize this risk and how to handle it when it happens. Many of our members have been operating for years without issues.
Is this legal in Australia? ▼
Yes, absolutely. We're simply providing betting education and alerts. You're placing legal bets with licensed Australian bookmakers. Nothing we do is illegal or against any rules.
What if I'm a complete beginner? ▼
Perfect! We include complete education covering everything from the basics to advanced strategies. Many of our most successful members started with zero experience. You just need to be willing to learn.
