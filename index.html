The Lightbulb Moment

Everything changed when I learned this

That's when I discovered +EV betting (positive expected value).

Turns out, sharp bettors don't bet on who they think will win. They bet when the odds are mathematically in their favor.

If a team has a 55% chance to win, but you're getting odds that imply only 50%? That's +EV. Do that hundreds of times, and the math guarantees profit.

I spent 3 months learning everything: expected value, arbitrage, closing line value, bankroll management, Kelly Criterion. I built spreadsheets. I tracked every single bet.
