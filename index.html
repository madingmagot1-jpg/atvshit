Everything You Need to Know

What exactly is +EV betting? ‚ñº

+EV (positive expected value) betting means betting when the odds are mathematically in your favour. For example, if a team has a 55% chance to win but the odds imply only 50%, that's +EV. Over hundreds of bets, this edge compounds into consistent profit. It's not gambling‚Äîit's maths.

How do the Discord alerts work? ‚ñº

Our system scans 15+ bookmakers 24/7. When it identifies a +EV bet or arb opportunity, you get an instant Discord notification (usually within 90 seconds). The alert includes: sport, match, bet type, recommended bookmaker, odds, and calculated EV or guaranteed profit percentage.

What's the difference between +EV betting and arbing? ‚ñº

+EV betting: You bet when odds are favourable. There's still variance (you can lose individual bets), but the maths ensures long-term profit.

Arbitrage: You bet both sides of a match on different bookmakers to guarantee profit regardless of outcome. Zero risk, but requires accounts on multiple books.
