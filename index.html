What exactly is +EV betting? ▼

+EV (positive expected value) betting means betting when the odds are mathematically in your favour. If a team has a 55% chance to win but the odds imply only 50%, that's +EV. Over hundreds of bets, this edge compounds into consistent profit. It's not gambling. It's maths.
